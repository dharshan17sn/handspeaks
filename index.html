Overview of the Project

Welcome to Handspeaks – a game-changing smartwatch that lets you control your devices with simple hand gestures. No buttons, no touchscreens – just you and your movements. This is more than just a smartwatch – it's the future of interaction, making technology feel more natural and intuitive.

Our system uses cutting-edge AI and sensor technology to understand your unique gestures. Whether you’re answering calls, changing music, or controlling apps, your hand movements are instantly recognized and translated into actions. The more you use it, the smarter it gets!

But we’re not stopping there. The Handspeaks project is just getting started. We’re working to integrate more devices, improve recognition accuracy, and expand the possibilities of gesture control. Imagine controlling your home, car, and more – all with just a flick of your wrist.

Handspeaks isn’t just about convenience; it’s about enhancing your experience with technology, making it more accessible, and putting you in control – effortlessly.

How to Track Hand Gestures in Real-Time

Our smartwatch tracks your hand gestures in real-time using advanced sensors like accelerometers and gyroscopes, combined with AI to translate movements into commands instantly. Watch the video to see how it works in action!
